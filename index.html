LoveEarth

Wendy Fajardo (HS Junior)

Game Developer in the Making

PITCH

LoveEarth is a fun beach clean-up game where you collect points by picking up trash while learning about climate change. As levels progress, the game gets more challenging, with hidden trash and cute animals along the way. What makes it special is its message—it's not just a game, but a way to raise awareness and inspire the next generation to care for the planet.

THEME

In LoveEarth, I focused on climate change, particularly littering, to raise awareness. The game combines fun and creativity to show how discarded clothing harms marine life. I used vibrant colors and a beach theme to make it visually appealing, with sea animals to highlight the issue. The gameplay is simple: players collect trash to earn points, but failing to clean up harms marine life, reinforcing the game's message about protecting the environment.

MY WHY

I created my game to be fun, engaging, and visually appealing, focusing on design to capture players' attention. The main theme is climate change, highlighting how trash harms the environment, especially the ocean. I wanted the game to be child-friendly, not childish, so younger players can learn about pollution and how to help. I also included resources to teach players how to reduce plastic use and make eco-friendly choices.
