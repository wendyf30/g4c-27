PITCH

In loveEarth you would be able to collect points as you are also collecting trash at the beach. this game is going to be the life of the party and you are going to be able to have a lot of fun and you won't even realize the time that has gone by as you play the game. in the game players will be able to learn about the issues of climate change and will also be able to partake and seeing cute animals andAs you continue the levels will get a little more harder as the trash will get further and maybe even not even seen.My game sets apart from a lot of other games because obviously it's something that I created and I had a vision for it to be something very different from many other people's but I think that obviously this game is Just envisioning an issue in the world that is very much unseen and I hope that with this game people are able to see it more and I don't think it's just some game I think it's also setting an example for future generations and telling them issues that they should be aware of.
